Payments

When you tap "Done" on an order, your customer gets a payment link via text. They pay online before pickup. The money goes directly to your bank account via Stripe — usually within 1–2 business days. You never have to chase anyone down.
ThreadDesk takes nothing. You pay $39/month flat and keep 100% of your revenue. Stripe charges their standard processing fee (around 2.9% + $0.30 per transaction) — that's Stripe's fee, not ours.
Yes. ThreadDesk doesn't force anything. You can mark any order as "paid in cash" and it'll be recorded in your history. Over time, most shops find customers actually prefer paying online — it's more convenient for everyone.

Pricing & cancellation

No contract, no minimum. ThreadDesk is month-to-month. Cancel anytime from your account settings or just text us and we'll handle it. No cancellation fees, no questions asked.
We'll export your full order history and customer records in a clean spreadsheet and send it to you before your account closes. Your data is yours — we don't hold it hostage.
We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee. If ThreadDesk doesn't make your life measurably easier in the first month, we'll refund your first payment. No arguing about it.
