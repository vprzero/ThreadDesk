Privacy Policy

Last updated January 1, 2025

What we collect

ThreadDesk collects the information you provide when setting up your account (name, phone number, pricing), and the customer order data entered through the intake system (customer phone numbers, order details, photos).

How we use it

  • To operate the ThreadDesk service on your behalf
  • To send automated messages to your customers (order confirmations, payment requests, review requests)
  • To generate your daily and weekly summary reports

What we never do

  • We never sell your data or your customers' data to anyone
  • We never store payment card numbers (all payments handled by Stripe)
  • We never use your data to advertise to your customers

Data retention

If you cancel ThreadDesk, we'll export your complete order and customer history as a spreadsheet and send it to you. Your data is deleted from our systems within 30 days of account closure.

Contact

Questions about your data? Email us at hello@threaddesk.com or use our contact form.
